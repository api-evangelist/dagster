Dagster's dbt integration enables teams to orchestrate SQL transformations as part of a larger, asset-driven pipeline by sharing dbt's asset-centric mental model. The integration supports flexible scheduling, observability, partitioning, and alerting, offering capabilities that go beyond dbt Cloud's built-in orchestration. Dagster remains open-source with self-deployment options while supporting diverse asset types beyond dbt including ML models and data ingestion tools.
